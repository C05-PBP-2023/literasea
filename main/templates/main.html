Our Features

Book Catalogue

The catalog of books available on Literasea. Users can see book details such as the title, author's name, book publisher, ISBN, and publication year. Users can filter the book catalog based on the publication year and publisher. In addition to viewing available books, users can also add books they want to purchase to the cart.

Book Review

Allows users to share their opinions about a book they've read. Users can write reviews covering various aspects of the book, including its story, writing style, and more. In addition, users can rate books and provide a rating out of five stars, offering a quick summary of their overall impression.

Q & A

Create questions about a book, whether related to the story or other aspects of the book. The questions can be answered by any writers registered on the page.

Book Tracker

Allowing users to revisit their most recently read books. With this feature, users can easily find the book's title and the last page they read, making it convenient for them to resume reading from where they left off.

Shopping Cart

To checkout the books from the cart. Users can also filter which books they want to purchase in the cart. Users will receive information about the buyer's name, purchase date, and the quantity of books bought. Additionally, users can also view their purchase history.
